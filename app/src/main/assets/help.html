AI Journal Companion — Help

This page explains how to use the app and what each feature does. It runs fully offline except for your local API (FastAPI + Ollama) on your computer.

1. Write & Analyze

  • Open Write tab.
  • Choose a model (e.g., gemma:2b) from the dropdown.
  • Enter your journal text and tap Analyze.
  • The app sends your text to the local backend and shows:
    • Emotion (JOY / SADNESS / ANGER / FEAR / DISGUST / SURPRISE / NEUTRAL)
    • Advice (one line)

If nothing returns, check your backend is running (FastAPI) and the emulator can reach http://10.0.2.2:8080.

2. Sort

  • Open History tab, pick a method: Bubble, Insertion or Selection.
  • Tap Sort to reorder entries by emotion (A→Z by emotion code).

3. Search

  • In History, choose a search method: BinaryTree, HashMap, or DoublyLinkedList.
  • Select Target Emotion (e.g., JOY) and tap Search.
  • Matched entries are highlighted.

4. Drag & Delete

  • Press and drag a journal card.
  • Drop it onto the bottom Drag here to delete bar to remove.

5. Chart

  • Open Chart tab to view a pie chart of emotion distribution.

6. Backend Setup (Quick)

  • Run your FastAPI/Ktor on your PC (port 8080), ensure Ollama model is available.
  • Android emulator reaches your host by http://10.0.2.2:8080.

FAQ

  • No response? Confirm backend is up; check port and firewall.
  • Error 422? The model output was not valid JSON. Try again or switch a model.
  • Which emotions are supported? JOY, SADNESS, ANGER, FEAR, DISGUST, SURPRISE, NEUTRAL.

Privacy: Your text is sent only to your local API on the same machine.
